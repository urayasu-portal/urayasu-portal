Everything you might need outside the parks, on one map. Pick the hotel you are staying at, then filter by need (buy diapers, eat late at night, withdraw cash with a foreign card, and more) or by store type to see the nearest places with walking times.

{{< facility-map >}}

## How to use

- **Pick your hotel** — the map recenters on it with a star marker and a 400 m guide circle. Each place shows "N-min walk from hotel" in its popup.
- **By need (coral buttons)** — choose from Diapers & baby, Bento & snacks, Late-night meal, Breakfast, Cash (foreign card), Laundry, Medicine, Clothes, Japanese food, Tax-free, and Ship luggage to show only matching places.
- **By type (lower buttons)** — filter by convenience, supermarket, pharmacy, food, café, 100-yen, electronics, clothing, laundry, post office, or malls (multi-select).
- **Open now** — shows only places open at the current time (a simple estimate based on listed hours).

Hours and walking times are estimates and may vary late at night, early morning, or on holidays. Check each store's official page ("Find stores" in the popup) for the latest information.
