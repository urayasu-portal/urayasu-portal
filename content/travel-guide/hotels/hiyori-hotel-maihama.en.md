Hiyori Hotel Maihama is a hotel in the Higashino district offering some genuinely thoughtful in-room amenities: a foot massager and shoe dryer in every guest room. An infused water server is available free of charge, and the buffet breakfast features 30+ items spanning Japanese and Western options — strong value for the price range. Parking is ¥1,000/night (40 spaces, first-come). A shuttle runs from Maihama Station South Exit stop C3 in approximately 10 minutes. This review covers strengths, weaknesses, and access from a local perspective. Prices and times are estimates based on 2025–2026 research.

## Best For

- **Guests whose feet hurt after a long day at the parks** — In-room foot massager in every room: a genuinely practical amenity after a full park day
- **Guests who want to dry shoes and park rain gear** — In-room shoe dryer in every room; also useful for drying wet items quickly in rainy seasons
- **Guests who want a satisfying buffet breakfast without the Official Hotel price tag** — 30+ item Japanese/Western buffet at a mid-range price point; notably well-stocked for the category

## Access

### To Tokyo Disneyland
Hotel-Maihama Station free shuttle ~10 min, then Disney Resort Line to Tokyo Disneyland Station.

### To Tokyo DisneySea
Hotel-Maihama Station free shuttle ~10 min, then Disney Resort Line to Tokyo DisneySea Station.

### To JR Station
Hotel shuttle to/from Maihama Station South Exit stop C3: approx. 10 min.

### To the Airport
From Maihama Station bus stop, airport limousine to Haneda: approx. 40 min, ¥1,300. Narita: approx. 75 min, ¥2,900 (confirm schedule before departure).

### To Central Tokyo
From Maihama Station, about 15 minutes to Tokyo Station on the JR Keiyo Line.

## Local Notes

Hiyori Hotel Maihama's in-room foot massager and shoe dryer combination is unusual enough to be a genuine differentiator. Most hotels in this price range provide basic amenities; Hiyori seems to have made a deliberate choice to target "guests who are physically tired after the parks." The buffet breakfast adding 30+ items further supports that hospitality-focused positioning.

The shuttle from Maihama Station South Exit stop C3 (~10 min) is the practical daily route. Parking at ¥1,000/night (40 spaces) is among the lower rates for hotel parking in this area, making it a reasonable option for driving guests.
