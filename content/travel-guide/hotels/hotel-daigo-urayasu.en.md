Hotel Daigo Urayasu is a small 31-room inn approximately 3 minutes on foot from Urayasu Station (Tokyo Metro Tozai Line). The most distinctive feature is its pricing: a **year-round flat rate** that does not change during summer holidays, Golden Week, or year-end periods when every other hotel in the area raises prices. Japanese-style rooms are available. A convenience store is directly below the hotel. 8 free parking spaces on a first-come basis. Check-in from 16:00. **Credit cards are not accepted — cash only.** Reference rate: ¥25,000 for 4 people. This review covers the hotel's strengths, weaknesses, and access. Prices and times are estimates based on 2025–2026 research.

## Best For

- **Guests visiting during peak periods who don't want to pay peak pricing** — Year-round flat rate means ¥25,000 for 4 people in Golden Week and summer holidays is the same as any other time
- **Guests who want to sleep in Japanese-style rooms (futon on tatami)** — Japanese-style rooms available — uncommon in the Urayasu hotel area
- **Guests arriving by car who need free parking near Urayasu Station** — 8 free parking spaces (first-come); contact in advance as availability is limited
- **Groups of 3–4 who want a low-cost per-person rate** — ¥25,000/4 people reference rate works out to ¥6,250 per person — extremely competitive for peak periods

## Must-Know Before Booking

- **CASH ONLY — credit cards and electronic payments are not accepted.** Bring sufficient Japanese yen in cash
- **Check-in from 16:00** — later than the typical 15:00 standard at area hotels; plan arrival accordingly
- **Check-out at 10:00** — earlier than some area hotels
- **Only 31 rooms** — book early, especially for peak periods despite the flat rate

## Access

### To Tokyo Disneyland (TDL)
Local bus from Urayasu Station to Maihama Station ~25-30 min (Y240), then Disney Resort Line to Tokyo Disneyland Station (no dedicated shuttle).

### To Tokyo DisneySea (TDS)
Local bus from Urayasu Station to Maihama Station ~25-30 min (Y240), then Disney Resort Line to Tokyo DisneySea Station (no dedicated shuttle).

### To JR Station
About 3 min on foot from Urayasu Station (Tokyo Metro Tozai Line). Transfer to JR lines via Nihonbashi, Otemachi (Tokyo Station area), and connecting stations. For Maihama/TDR, the bus from Urayasu Station is the practical route.

### To the Airport
No dedicated limousine stop. To Haneda: approx. 60 min via Urayasu → Nihonbashi → Toei Asakusa Line or Keikyu Line. To Narita: approx. 80–90 min.

### To Central Tokyo
Urayasu Station → Tokyo Metro Tozai Line: approx. 16–18 min to Nihonbashi/Otemachi.

**Year-Round Flat Rate (the defining feature)**
Hotel Daigo Urayasu does not use dynamic pricing. The rate is the same regardless of whether it's a summer holiday, Golden Week, or year-end. For 4 people: reference rate ¥25,000. A family of 4 in peak summer pays the same as in February.

**Japanese-Style Rooms**
Tatami-floor rooms with futon bedding available. Japanese-style rooms are rare in the Urayasu hotel area, making this one of very few options for guests who specifically want this experience.

**Convenience Store Below the Hotel**
A convenience store directly on the ground floor below the hotel. Late-night and early-morning access without going outside.

**8 Free Parking Spaces**
Free parking for guests (8 spaces, first-come). Contact the hotel in advance if parking is required — 8 spaces fills up quickly.

## Local Notes

Hotel Daigo Urayasu is a genuinely unusual option in the TDR area: **flat-rate pricing throughout the year, Japanese-style rooms, cash only, and free parking.** Each of these is uncommon individually; all four together makes this hotel a specific type of recommendation.

For families planning a summer or Golden Week Disney trip who are paying very close attention to total cost, the flat-rate pricing can be the most important factor. Most hotels in this area charge 2–3× their off-peak rates during peak periods; Hotel Daigo simply doesn't. The ¥25,000 for 4 people reference rate may be among the lowest per-person costs you'll find anywhere near TDR during those periods.

The practical constraints — cash only, 16:00 check-in, 31 rooms only — require active planning. Bring cash. Don't plan to arrive before 16:00 without arranging luggage storage. Book early because peak availability disappears.
