Comfort Suites Tokyo Bay (Choice Hotels). A free shuttle to TDL and TDS runs daily; when the park closes by 22:00, the shuttle operates until 30 minutes after closing — a practical design feature for families who want to stay until the end. With 312 rooms, children through elementary school 6th grade sleeping free (up to 2 per room), free Wi-Fi throughout, a breakfast buffet, and a free self-cloakroom (available before check-in), this is a well-regarded family-friendly mid-range hotel in the Shin-Urayasu area. This review covers strengths, weaknesses, and access. Prices and times are estimates based on 2025–2026 research.

## Best For

- **Families who want to stay until park closing without worrying about the shuttle** — When park closes by 22:00, the shuttle runs until 30 min after closing; high chance of making it home even if you stay to the end
- **Families with elementary-school children who want to control costs** — Children through 6th grade sleep free (up to 2 per room); simple cost calculation for the whole family
- **Guests with lots of luggage who want a smooth check-in** — Free self-cloakroom available before check-in; drop off bags immediately upon arrival and head straight to the parks

## May Not Suit

- **Guests wanting a large communal bath** — No large bath or hot spring (in-room bathroom only)
- **Guests who need a reliable late-night convenience store** — In-house convenience store hours require final confirmation
- **Guests on days when the park closes after 22:00** — Shuttle schedule for park closings after 22:00 requires separate confirmation

## Access

### To Tokyo Disneyland
Free hotel shuttle bus (runs until ~30 min after park closing).

### To Tokyo DisneySea
Free hotel shuttle bus (runs until ~30 min after park closing).

### To JR Station
From Shin-Urayasu Station South Exit bus terminal, stop D: Tokyo Baycity Bus Routes 3 or 23 (bound for Sogo Koen), approx. 10 min to "Akemi 5-chome" stop, then 1 min on foot.

### To the Airport
From "Bayside Hotel Area" bus stop (approx. 5 min walk): approx. 47–52 min to Haneda Airport, ¥1,300 (Tokyo Airport Transport / Keiseibus Chiba West; some departures may not stop — confirm). Narita also from same stop.

### To Central Tokyo
From Shin-Urayasu Station, about 20 minutes to Tokyo Station on the JR Keiyo Line or Musashino Line.

## Local Notes

Comfort Suites Tokyo Bay's distinguishing feature is the "shuttle until 30 minutes after park closing" design. Many Shin-Urayasu hotels reduce shuttle frequency after closing, but knowing that shuttle availability is maintained for a defined period after closing gives real peace of mind.

"Akemi 5-chome" bus stop is 1 min away, placing it in the same service zone as Hyatt Regency Tokyo Bay and Mitsui Garden Hotel Prana. The mid-range pricing for the Shin-Urayasu area (standard period approx. ¥15,000–35,000), combined with the free self-cloakroom, free Wi-Fi, and breakfast buffet, covers the basics well for guests who want an international chain name without sacrificing value.
