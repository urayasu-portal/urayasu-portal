MyStays Hotel Shin-Urayasu Conference Center is a 175-room business hotel in the Shin-Urayasu area. The 24-hour coin laundry at just ¥200 per wash/dry cycle is among the cheapest in the area — a useful amenity for extended stays and families. A conference center is on site. The TDR shuttle operates only in the evenings (approximately 3 departures between 21:00–22:00, shared with other MyStays properties) — unlike hotels with all-day shuttles. No in-house convenience store. This review covers the hotel's strengths, weaknesses, and access. Prices and times are estimates based on 2025–2026 research.

## Best For

- **Extended stay guests who do laundry frequently** — 24-hour coin laundry at just ¥200/cycle; among the cheapest in the Shin-Urayasu hotel area
- **Business travelers and conference attendees** — On-site conference center; the only hotel in the Shin-Urayasu area with a dedicated conference facility in this category
- **Budget-conscious guests who just need a clean, functional room** — Starting at approx. ¥7,500, covering the basics without extras

## May Not Suit

- **Guests who need a shuttle to TDR from the morning** — Evening-only shuttle (approx. 21:00–22:00 departures back to hotel); for morning park access, local bus or taxi is needed
- **Guests who need a convenience store for late-night shopping** — No in-house convenience store; nearest external option requires confirmation
- **Guests wanting a large communal bath** — No large bath

## Local Notes

MyStays Shin-Urayasu Conference Center occupies a niche as "the cheapest extended-stay base with laundry access and conference facilities in Shin-Urayasu." It's not a hotel optimized for TDR visits — the evening-only shuttle is the clearest signal of that. For guests who primarily need a clean, affordable base close to Shin-Urayasu Station with very cheap coin laundry, it fits well.

The ¥200 laundry rate is a genuine local standout. Most hotels in the area charge ¥300–¥500 per cycle; ¥200 saves noticeable money on a multi-night stay.
