Maihama View Hotel (formerly Tokyo Bay Maihama Hotel, rebranded and reopened in 2025 under HULIC management as a Tokyo Disney Resort Official Hotel) stands out within the Official Hotel category for having a large communal bath — "Spa Rose" — which is uncommon among Official Hotels. A free shuttle connects to Maihama Station, Ikspiari, and Bayside Station in approximately 5 minutes. Coin laundry was under construction during the research period — confirm current availability. The airport limousine stop situation post-rebrand also requires verification. This review covers the hotel's strengths, weaknesses, and access. Prices and times are estimates based on 2025–2026 research.

## Best For

- **Guests who want both Official Hotel Disney benefits AND a large communal bath** — "Spa Rose" large bath is available (paid; uncommon among Official Hotels); a combination hard to find elsewhere in this tier
- **Guests wanting quick shuttle access to Maihama, Ikspiari, and Bayside** — Free shuttle in approx. 5 min; the fastest connection to Maihama Station and Ikspiari among this hotel's peer group

## May Not Suit

- **Guests who rely on coin laundry** — Coin laundry is being newly installed on 2F (men's powder room side); confirm completion status on the official website before booking
- **Guests who rely on the airport limousine stop** — Limousine stop location post-rebrand requires confirmation on the official website
- **Guests who want the most established Official Hotel with the longest track record** — Rebrand is recent (2025); some services are still settling in

## Access

### To Tokyo Disneyland
~18-min walk to TDL, or free shuttle + monorail.

### To Tokyo DisneySea
Free shuttle (~5 min) + Disney Resort Line from Bayside Station to Tokyo DisneySea Station.

### To JR Station
Free shuttle to Maihama Station: approx. 5 min (also to Bayside Station and Ikspiari on the same shuttle).

### To the Airport
Airport limousine bus stop: confirm current situation on the official website post-rebrand (the former hotel had a limousine stop; verify continuity after the 2025 rebrand).

### To Central Tokyo
From Maihama Station, about 15 minutes to Tokyo Station on the JR Keiyo Line.

## Local Notes

The main story of Maihama View Hotel is the 2025 rebrand — from Tokyo Bay Maihama Hotel to Maihama View Hotel under HULIC — while retaining the TDR Official Hotel designation. The key elements that make this hotel distinctive are the "Spa Rose" large bath (unusual among Official Hotels) and the 5-minute shuttle to Maihama Station/Ikspiari (among the fastest in the area).

For the 2025–2026 period, a few details remain in transition: coin laundry status, the limousine stop location, and post-rebrand service updates. Check the official website for current details before booking. If the large bath is your primary draw and you want Official Hotel Disney benefits, this is a strong candidate — once the transition details are confirmed.
