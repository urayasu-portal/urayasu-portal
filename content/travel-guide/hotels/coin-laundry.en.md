Coin laundry is the quiet hero of a multi-night Disney trip with kids: pack half the clothes, wash mid-trip, and use the suitcase space you saved for souvenirs. All the hotels below have laundry machines that staying guests can actually use — we exclude hotels where the laundry is under construction, staff-service only, or "please ask" vague.

One honest note: machine counts are limited even at big hotels, and the after-park evening rush is real. If laundry is central to your packing strategy, a hotel with more machines beats a cheaper one with a single washer — the hotel pages note what we know about each.
