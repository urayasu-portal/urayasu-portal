After a park day with small kids, the difference between "there's a shop downstairs" and "someone has to go back out" is enormous. This list covers hotels with a shop inside the building — the label is deliberately honest: it ranges from genuine 24-hour convenience stores at a few hotels to hotel gift shops that stock drinks, snacks, toiletries and baby basics alongside the souvenirs.

Price-wise, in-house shops usually run a little higher than a street konbini, which is the trade you make for slippers-distance access. Each hotel's page describes what the shop actually is; for everything the hotel can't cover, keep our [Urayasu shopping & essentials map](/en/travel-guide/urayasu-map/) handy — it lists the nearest convenience store, pharmacy, ATM and coin laundry around each area.
