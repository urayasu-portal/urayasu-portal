HOTEL LiVEMAX Kasai-Ekimae is about a 3-minute walk from Tokyo Metro Tozai Line Kasai Station. A LiVEMAX-chain business hotel with 57 rooms, it offers rooms with a microwave and shower toilet, a café-equipped coin laundry ("MAX WASH & CAFE") and free Wi-Fi — simple and practical. Tokyo Disney Resort is about 20 minutes away by Keisei bus, and Haneda Airport about 35 minutes. Prices and times are estimates based on 2025–2026 research.

## Access

### To the Parks (TDL / TDS)
About 20 min by Keisei bus from Kasai Station to TDR (about 13 min by car).

### To the Nearest Station
About 3 min on foot from Tokyo Metro Tozai Line Kasai Station.

### To the Airport
About 35 min to Haneda Airport.

### To Central Tokyo
About 20 min to Otemachi / 18 min to Nihonbashi on the Tozai Line (one stop to Urayasu).
