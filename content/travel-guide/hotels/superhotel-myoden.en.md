Super Hotel Tozai Line Ichikawa-Myoden is about a 2-minute walk from Tokyo Metro Tozai Line Myoden Station. True to the Super Hotel chain, it offers a free breakfast buffet centered on organic vegetables and freshly baked bread, along with a coin laundry, parking and luggage storage. The Tozai Line provides easy access to Urayasu and central Tokyo; TDR is reached by transferring toward Maihama. Prices and times are estimates based on 2025–2026 research.

## Best For

- **Guests who care about breakfast** — Free buffet with organic vegetables and freshly baked bread
- **Guests who want to be near the station** — 2 min from Tozai Line Myoden Station
- **Guests moving via the Tozai Line** — About 7 min to Urayasu, about 25 min to Otemachi

## May Not Suit

- **Guests who want a large bath** — No large bath (all rooms have a unit bath)
- **Guests who want a direct free TDR shuttle** — No direct shuttle (via Nishi-Funabashi to Maihama)

---

## Access

### To the Parks (TDL / TDS)
Myoden → Nishi-Funabashi → JR Keiyo Line to Maihama, then the Disney Resort Line to the parks (no direct shuttle).

### To the Nearest Station
About 2 min on foot from Tokyo Metro Tozai Line Myoden Station.

### To the Airport
About 60 min to Haneda Airport.

### To Central Tokyo
About 25 min to Otemachi on the Tozai Line (about 7 min to Urayasu).

Available.

Available.
