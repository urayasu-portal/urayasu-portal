Hotel Il Fiore Kasai is a 3-minute walk from Tokyo Metro Tozai Line Kasai Station. With 128 rooms ranging from Hollywood twins and twins to family rooms for up to 4, plus free co-sleeping for elementary-age children and under, it is well suited to families. It has a coin laundry, parking and a free-drink service from 15:00–20:00, with access to Tokyo Disney Resort in about 25 minutes. Prices and times are estimates based on 2025–2026 research.

## Best For

- **Families with children** — Family rooms for up to 4; free co-sleeping for elementary-age children and under
- **Guests who want to be near the station** — 3 min from Tozai Line Kasai Station
- **Guests doing laundry on longer stays** — Coin laundry, plus free drinks 15:00–20:00

## May Not Suit

- **Guests who want a direct free TDR shuttle** — No direct shuttle (Keisei bus or train)
- **Guests who want a large bath** — Not listed on the official site; please confirm

## Access

### To Tokyo Disneyland (TDL)
Keisei bus from Kasai Station, approx. 20-25 min.

### To Tokyo DisneySea (TDS)
Keisei bus from Kasai Station, approx. 20-25 min.

### To the Nearest Station
About 3 min on foot from Tokyo Metro Tozai Line Kasai Station.

### To the Airport
About 50–60 min to Haneda Airport by train/bus connections.

### To Central Tokyo
About 20 min to Otemachi / 18 min to Nihonbashi on the Tozai Line (one stop to Urayasu).
