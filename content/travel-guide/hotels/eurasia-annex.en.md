Maihama Eurasia Annex is designed around the atmosphere of Nice and the Côte d'Azur in southern France. Guests receive discounted access to the natural hot spring and large bath at the main Maihama Eurasia building (a 50% discount campaign was running April–July 2026; other periods vary — confirm on the official website). The shuttle from Maihama Station takes about 15 minutes. Check-out is 11:00. This review covers the hotel's strengths, weaknesses, and access from a local perspective. Prices and times are estimates based on 2025–2026 research.

## Best For

- **Guests who want to enjoy a natural hot spring at a budget-friendly rate** — Discounted access to the main Eurasia building's natural hot spring available (50% campaign Apr–Jul 2026; other periods vary — confirm in advance)
- **Guests looking for a unique design hotel near Maihama** — "Nice/Côte d'Azur" theme creates a distinctive southern European resort atmosphere
- **Guests prioritizing value and a pleasant stay over luxury** — Price range of ¥8,000–¥20,000 with discounted hot spring access offers strong value

## May Not Suit

- **Guests wanting to walk from Maihama Station** — About 15 min from Maihama Station by shuttle; not walkable
- **Guests wanting a 24-hour convenience store nearby** — Convenience store access requires confirmation
- **Guests wanting a late check-out** — 11:00 check-out is earlier than many area hotels

## Access

### To Tokyo Disneyland
Hotel-Maihama Station free shuttle ~15 min, then Disney Resort Line to Tokyo Disneyland Station.

### To Tokyo DisneySea
Hotel-Maihama Station free shuttle ~15 min, then Disney Resort Line to Tokyo DisneySea Station.

### To JR Station
Hotel shuttle to Maihama Station: approx. 15 min.

### To the Airport
From Maihama Station bus stop, airport limousine bus to Haneda: approx. 40 min, ¥1,300. Narita: approx. 75 min, ¥2,900 (advance reservation recommended during peak seasons).

### To Central Tokyo
From Maihama Station, about 15 minutes to Tokyo Station on the JR Keiyo Line.

## Local Notes

Maihama Eurasia Annex's identity is defined by the combination of the southern France theme and access to the main Eurasia building's natural hot spring at a discounted rate. While both Emion Tokyo Bay (Partner Hotel) and Maihama Eurasia (main building) have natural hot springs in the area, the Annex offers a more affordable entry point into the hot spring experience.

Key points to confirm before booking: the current hot spring discount rate and conditions (as the campaign has changed), shuttle access to the main building, and the 11:00 check-out time.
