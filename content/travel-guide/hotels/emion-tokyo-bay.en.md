Hotel Emion Tokyo Bay is the only Tokyo Disney Resort Partner Hotel with a natural hot spring — a saline spring producing amber-colored water. The large communal bath is included in the room rate. With 584 rooms including "Family Wonder" rooms for up to 6 guests, Welcome Baby certification for families with infants, and free bike rental, it covers a wider range of guest needs than other Partner Hotels. This review covers strengths, weaknesses, and access from a local perspective. Prices and times are estimates based on 2025–2026 research.

## Best For

- **Guests who want to soak in a natural hot spring after a long day at the parks** — The only natural hot spring (saline spring, amber-colored) among the four Partner Hotels, included in the room rate
- **Families with infants and babies** — Welcome Baby certified; baby bath, sterilizer, and infant-specific amenities available; pregnancy-friendly services also offered
- **Large groups of up to 6** — "Family Wonder" rooms accommodate up to 6 guests; suitable for three-generation families
- **Guests who want a free bike option** — Free bike rental is available (reservation recommended during peak periods)

## May Not Suit

- **Guests who want to walk directly from Shin-Urayasu Station** — About 7 min from the station by hotel shuttle, or approx. 15 min on foot; no deck connection
- **Guests wanting the absolute lowest price** — A step above the price range of budget Partner Hotels due to the hot spring

## Access

### To Tokyo Disneyland
Partner Hotel free shuttle, approx. 15 min (every 10-20 min in the morning).

### To Tokyo DisneySea
Partner Hotel free shuttle, approx. 15 min (every 10-20 min in the morning).

### To JR Station
Hotel shuttle to Shin-Urayasu Station: approx. 7 min. Or approx. 15 min on foot from the station's North Exit A2.

### To the Airport
From "Shin-Urayasu Station" bus stop (South Exit, stop H; approx. 7 min by hotel shuttle + 3 min walk): Haneda approx. 40–45 min, ¥1,300 (Tokyo Airport Transport / Keiseibus Chiba West; advance reservation recommended during peak seasons). Narita also available.

### To Central Tokyo
From Shin-Urayasu Station, about 20 minutes to Tokyo Station on the JR Keiyo Line.

## Facilities & Services

**Natural Hot Spring "Emion Onsen" (the defining advantage)**
The only natural hot spring among the four Partner Hotels. A saline spring producing amber-colored water — believed to promote circulation and skin softness. Separate men's and women's baths with indoor and outdoor tubs. Included in the room rate.

**Guest Rooms (584 rooms, up to 6 guests)**
Wide variety of room types. "Family Wonder" rooms accommodate up to 6 guests (3 double beds + sofa, 60 m²). Standard rooms also include welcome baby amenities and infant-specific amenities on request.

**Welcome Baby Certification**
Baby bath with temperature check device, sterilizer, infant carriers, and other facilities are prepared. Staff are trained in infant care support. Pregnancy-friendly services available.

**Breakfast**
Buffet breakfast available (Japanese and Western; includes kid-friendly menu items).

**Coin Laundry**
Available in-house (fee applies).

**Convenience Store**
Nearest convenience store: approx. 5–10 min on foot (confirm at front desk). MONA Shin-Urayasu shopping center within reach.

**Free Bike Rental**
Free for guests (reservation recommended during peak periods). An active option for exploring the Shin-Urayasu area.

**Parking**
Available (fee applies; confirm at booking).

**Luggage**
Storage before check-in and after check-out at the bell captain desk (1F lobby). As a Partner Hotel, station-to-hotel delivery uses the paid "Bon Voyage" counter by JR Maihama Station (about **¥800 per item**, until around 15:00). See [how Maihama luggage delivery works](/en/travel-guide/hotels/luggage/).

**Wi-Fi**
Free Wi-Fi in all rooms.

## Local Notes

The reason Emion stands out among the four Partner Hotels is the **natural hot spring alone**. Most of the "Want to soak in a hot spring after the parks" demand in the Shin-Urayasu area points here. Brighton wins on station access, Oriental wins on shuttle frequency and in-house convenience store, but only Emion offers a natural hot spring bath included in the room rate — a decisive advantage.

The Welcome Baby certification and large-group room options (up to 6 people) also make it a top pick for families traveling with infants or three-generation groups. Free bike rental is a pleasant bonus rarely seen among TDR-area hotels.

The access from Shin-Urayasu Station (7 min shuttle or 15 min walk) is not the most convenient, but many guests find the hot spring well worth that trade-off.
