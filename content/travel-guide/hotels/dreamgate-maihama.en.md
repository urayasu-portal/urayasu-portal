Dreamgate Maihama has an access advantage that stands alone in the area: **both the Main building and Annex are directly connected to JR Maihama Station** — Main to the left of the ticket gate, Annex to the right. A NewDays convenience store is inside the hotel (6:30–23:00). Children 6 and under sleep free. Both buildings have 3 coin laundry machines (with room availability viewable on the in-room TV). Ground-floor Saizeriya is available for breakfast. This review covers the hotel's strengths, weaknesses, and access. Prices and times are estimates based on 2025–2026 research.

## Best For

- **Guests arriving by JR Keiyo Line who don't want to haul luggage through crowds** — Directly connected to Maihama Station; step off the train, walk through the gate, enter the hotel with heavy suitcases
- **Guests who want the fastest possible Maihama Station connection** — Among all hotels in the area, no hotel is more directly integrated with Maihama Station
- **Families with children 6 and under** — Children 6 and under sleep free (same room, sharing existing beds)
- **Families who value being steps from the station and a convenience store** — NewDays inside the hotel plus a direct station connection make arrivals and supply runs effortless

## May Not Suit

- **Guests who need a 24-hour convenience store** — NewDays inside the hotel is open 6:30–23:00 (not 24 hours)
- **Guests wanting a late check-out** — 11:00 check-out is earlier than most area hotels
- **Guests who prefer a larger hotel with more room types** — Two smaller buildings rather than one large property

## Access

### To Tokyo Disneyland
Directly connected to JR Maihama Station, then Disney Resort Line to Tokyo Disneyland Station.

### To Tokyo DisneySea
Directly connected to JR Maihama Station, then Disney Resort Line to Tokyo DisneySea Station.

### To JR Station
**Direct connection** to JR Maihama Station. Main building left of the ticket gate, Annex right of the ticket gate. No exterior walk needed even in rain.

### To the Airport
From Maihama Station bus stop (steps from the hotel exit): Haneda approx. 40 min, ¥1,300. Narita approx. 75 min, ¥2,900 (advance reservation recommended during peak seasons).

### To Central Tokyo
From Maihama Station, about 15 minutes to Tokyo Station on the JR Keiyo Line (direct line).

**Direct Station Connection (the defining feature)**
Main building: directly connected to the left of Maihama Station's ticket gate. Annex: directly connected to the right. Two buildings that literally flank the station. The only hotel in the entire TDR area with this kind of integration with Maihama Station.

**NewDays In-House (6:30–23:00)**
JR East's NewDays convenience store inside the hotel (hours 6:30–23:00). Covers morning coffee and breakfast pickup before the parks, and post-park snacks on return. Not 24-hour — note the 23:00 closing.

**Operated by JR-East Hotels**
Run by the JR-East hotel group and built directly into Maihama Station — the defining convenience of this hotel.

**Children 6 and Under Sleep Free**
Children in 1st grade and below sleep free in the same room (sharing existing beds). Practical for families with young children.

**3 Coin Laundry Machines per Building**
3 machines in each building. Room availability viewable on the in-room TV without needing to walk to the laundry room first — a convenience detail.

**Breakfast (Ground-Floor Saizeriya)**
Saizeriya Italian family restaurant on the ground floor. Open daily (confirm hours on official website). Morning set menus typically available at Saizeriya from early morning — a budget-friendly breakfast option steps from the hotel entrance.

**Parking**
Confirm details on official website.

**Luggage**
Free guest-only lockers in the main building (usable from the morning of your stay and after check-out) and a self-service cloak room in the annex — no fee. There is no station baggage-delivery service. See [luggage storage & delivery near the parks](/en/travel-guide/hotels/luggage/).

**Wi-Fi**
Free Wi-Fi in all rooms.

## Local Notes

Dreamgate Maihama's station connection is its entire identity as a hotel recommendation. "Walk off the Keiyo Line, enter the hotel, wake up, walk into Maihama Station, board the Disney Resort Line" — the entire chain of transit from Tokyo to the parks is as streamlined as it gets. For guests who ride trains and want zero walking between train and hotel, nothing in the area matches this.

The Saizeriya ground-floor breakfast option is also appreciated by guests who want a hot budget breakfast without a hotel buffet premium. NewDays opening at 6:30 covers early morning coffee and a quick grab-and-go option.

The main practical notes: NewDays closes at 23:00 (not 24 hours), and check-out is 11:00. Plan around these if late nights at the park are in your itinerary.
