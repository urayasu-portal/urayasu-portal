Premium hotel MONday Maihama View I opened in December 2025. All 57 rooms are spacious (up to about 56㎡), comfortably fitting 4–6 guests in an apartment-style layout. The hotel has a large bath with sauna and runs a free shuttle bus to Tokyo Disney Resort®. It is located in Minami-Kasai, Edogawa, with bus access from Kasai-Rinkai-Koen Station. Prices and times are estimates based on 2025–2026 research.

## Best For

- **Families and groups wanting space** — All rooms up to ~56㎡, 4–6 guests
- **Guests who want a large bath** — Large bath with sauna (5:00–01:00)
- **Guests heading to TDR by free shuttle** — Free shuttle bus to Tokyo Disney Resort

## May Not Suit

- **Guests who want to walk from the station** — Bus from Kasai-Rinkai-Koen Station is the practical route
- **Guests set on staying inside Urayasu City** — Located in Minami-Kasai, Edogawa (Urayasu vicinity)

## Access

### To Tokyo Disneyland
Free shuttle bus to TDR (bus ~7 min from Kasai-Rinkai-Koen Station).

### To Tokyo DisneySea
Free shuttle bus to TDR (bus ~7 min from Kasai-Rinkai-Koen Station).

### To the Nearest Station
JR Keiyo Line Kasai-Rinkai-Koen Station (by bus). Maihama is close on the Keiyo Line.

### To the Airport
About 60 min to Haneda Airport via Tokyo Station.

### To Central Tokyo
About 15 min from Kasai-Rinkai-Koen Station to Tokyo Station on the JR Keiyo Line.
