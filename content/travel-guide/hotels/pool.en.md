A pool day is the classic answer to the mid-trip energy crash — a morning swim, a slow lunch, and everyone is human again by evening. Pools are genuinely rare in the Tokyo Disney Resort area compared to shuttle buses and coin laundry, so this is a short list: only the hotels where our database confirms a pool, with notes for paid entry, heated water, or indoor-and-outdoor setups.

Fair warning from the fine print: pool fees, age rules and operating hours change often, and some pools close seasonally or for private events. Treat this page as your shortlist, then verify the current conditions on the hotel's page and official site — especially if the pool is the reason you're booking.
