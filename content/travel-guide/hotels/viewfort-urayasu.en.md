A business hotel about 1-minute walk from Urayasu Station on the Tokyo Metro Tozai Line (express stop), offering some of the most convenient central Tokyo access in the area. The buffet breakfast (7:00–9:30) is well-regarded, with fresh-baked croissants as a signature item. A convenience store is at the back of the hotel, and parking is available by reservation (¥1,100/night, height limit 1.55m). This review covers strengths, weaknesses, and access from a local perspective. Prices and times are estimates based on 2025–2026 research.

## Best For

- **Guests wanting a buffet breakfast and good value** — Buffet from 7:00–9:30 with fresh-baked croissants; fuels you up before heading to the parks
- **Guests prioritizing central Tokyo access** — 1-minute walk to Urayasu Station; about 16 min to Nihonbashi and 18 min to Otemachi (Tokyo Station area) on the Tozai Line express
- **Guests who need parking** — Hotel parking available (¥1,100/night, advance phone reservation required, height limit 1.55m)

## May Not Suit

- **Guests wanting a direct shuttle to TDR** — No free shuttle (approx. 25–30 min by local bus; bus stop about 2 min from Urayasu Station)
- **Guests looking for a large communal bath** — No large bath
- **Guests arriving in tall SUVs or minivans** — Hotel parking has a 1.55m height limit (confirm before booking)

## Access

### To the Parks (TDL / TDS)
From Urayasu Station bus stop (approx. 2 min via pedestrian overpass), Tokyo Baycity Bus Route 9 to Maihama Station area: approx. 25–30 min (¥240 adults / ¥120 children). Not eligible for TDR official benefits.

### To JR Station
About 1-minute walk to Urayasu Station (Tokyo Metro Tozai Line). Transfer to JR lines via Nihonbashi, Otemachi, and connecting stations.

### To the Airport
No dedicated limousine stop. To Haneda: approx. 60 min via Urayasu → Nihonbashi → Toei Asakusa Line or Keikyu Line. To Narita: approx. 80–90 min via the same route.

### To Central Tokyo
Urayasu Station → Tokyo Metro Tozai Line express: approx. 16 min to Nihonbashi, approx. 18 min to Otemachi (adjacent to Tokyo Station).
