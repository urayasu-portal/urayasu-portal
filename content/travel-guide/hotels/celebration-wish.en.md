Tokyo Disneyland Celebration Hotel: Wish is a value-type Disney Hotel operated by Tokyo Disney Resort, themed around "dreams and fantasy" inspired by Tokyo Disneyland. Standard rooms come with 4 beds — the most affordable Disney Hotel option that still includes Happy Entry early admission for families and groups sharing one room. This review covers strengths, weaknesses, and access from a local perspective. Prices and times are estimates based on 2025–2026 research.

## Best For

- **Guests who want a Disney Hotel but need to manage costs** — Among the most affordable Disney Hotels operated by TDR, with Happy Entry included
- **Families and groups of 3–4 sharing one room** — Standard and Superior rooms come with 4 beds (2 regular + 2 daybed); no extra charge for sleeping 4
- **Guests who don't prioritize being near the parks** — Free shuttle (approx. 20 min) to the parks; better value and room comfort over proximity

## May Not Suit

- **Guests wanting to walk from Shin-Urayasu Station with luggage** — Bus from Shin-Urayasu Station (Route 11, approx. 15 min) is the practical option; walking is far
- **Guests arriving from Narita Airport** — No direct Narita limousine; train connections required
- **Guests wanting a large communal bath or hot spring** — No large bath or hot spring
- **Guests wanting a late check-out** — 11:00 check-out is earlier than most TDR hotels (which are typically 12:00)

## Access

### To Tokyo Disneyland (TDL)
Free shuttle bus, approx. 20 min (15-min early entry, all rooms).

### To Tokyo DisneySea (TDS)
Free shuttle bus, approx. 20 min (15-min early entry, all rooms).

### To JR Station
From **Shin-Urayasu Station South Exit stop D, take Route 11 "Symbol Road Line" (bound for Sogo Koen) to "Bayside Hotel Area" stop**, then 2 min on foot. Approx. 15 min, ¥147 IC / ¥150 cash. Bus is essential as walking is far.

### To the Airport
From "Bayside Hotel Area" stop (approx. 2 min from hotel) to Haneda Airport: approx. 60 min, ¥1,300 (Tokyo Airport Transport / Keiseibus Chiba West; advance reservation recommended during peak seasons). **No direct limousine to Narita.** For Narita, use JR from Shin-Urayasu Station.

### To Central Tokyo
From Shin-Urayasu Station, about 20 minutes to Tokyo Station on the JR Keiyo Line.

## Facilities & Services

{{< hotel-facilities >}}

**Guest Rooms (4 beds standard)**
"Dreams and fantasy" theme with murals of Disney characters enjoying park attractions covering the walls. Standard and Superior rooms: 2 regular beds + 2 daybed = 4-person bed use (up to 2 additional children sharing). Corner rooms accommodate 3 guests.

**Happy Entry**
Disney Hotel guests enter the park 15 minutes before general admission via the dedicated hotel entrance. Park ticket required separately.

**Breakfast**
Breakfast plan: simple boxed meal and small water bottle served in the 1F lounge. Drink bar available 14:00–24:00 (not during breakfast hours). "Snacks & Sundries" in-house shop on 1F has a wide selection of rice balls, sandwiches, and packed meals.

**Coin Laundry**
Available on 5F for hotel guests. Suitable for extended stays and families.

**Convenience Store**
Lawson within 5 min on foot of the hotel, open late. For supermarkets, take the bus to MONA Shin-Urayasu (Aeon Style, approx. 15 min toward Shin-Urayasu Station).

**Parking**
¥2,000/night (per standard car, cash only). Available from 12:00 on check-in day to 12:00 on check-out day; in/out allowed.

**Luggage Storage**
Guest services counter in the lobby handles pre-check-in and post-check-out luggage. Baggage delivery between Disney Hotels also available.

**Wi-Fi**
Free Wi-Fi in rooms.

## Local Notes

Tokyo Disneyland Celebration Hotel (Wish and Discover, two buildings) sits in the position of "operated by TDR but located away from the parks, with reduced prices." Happy Entry is included, but the 20-minute shuttle distance is a clearly different experience from park-adjacent or connected hotels.

That said, "a family of 4 in one room at a Disney Hotel" is a combination that's genuinely hard to find elsewhere in the TDR lineup. Four people in two rooms costs more than sleeping 4 in one room — the practical cost-effectiveness here is real. Key points to know before booking: no Narita limousine, 11:00 check-out, and bus access from Shin-Urayasu Station (about 15 min).

## Price Guide

Standard period: approx. ¥20,000–30,000 for 3 guests per room. Peak periods (Golden Week, summer, year-end) may exceed ¥44,000 (dynamic pricing). Among the most affordable Disney Hotels. Check the latest availability and rates on booking sites.
