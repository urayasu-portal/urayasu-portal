Hotel Il Fiore Kasai ANNEX is the annex of Hotel Il Fiore Kasai, a 3-minute walk from Tokyo Metro Tozai Line Kasai Station. It offers doubles, Hollywood twins, standard twins for up to 4, and deluxe rooms for up to 6 — handy for larger groups. There is a 1F coin laundry and a free-drink corner, with access to Tokyo Disney Resort in about 20 minutes. Prices and times are estimates based on 2025–2026 research.

## Best For

- **Larger groups** — Deluxe rooms for up to 6
- **Guests who want station-close and low cost** — 3 min from Kasai Station, from ¥5,500 / 2 guests
- **Guests doing laundry on longer stays** — 1F coin laundry and free-drink corner

## May Not Suit

- **Guests who want a large bath** — No large bath (in-room bath only)
- **Guests who want a direct free TDR shuttle** — No direct shuttle (Keisei bus or train)

## Access

### To Tokyo Disneyland (TDL)
Keisei bus from Kasai Station, approx. 20 min.

### To Tokyo DisneySea (TDS)
Keisei bus from Kasai Station, approx. 20 min.

### To the Nearest Station
About 3 min on foot from Tokyo Metro Tozai Line Kasai Station.

### To the Airport
About 50–60 min to Haneda Airport by train/bus connections.

### To Central Tokyo
About 20 min to Otemachi / 18 min to Nihonbashi on the Tozai Line (one stop to Urayasu).
