Four Stories Hotel is a small boutique hotel with 26 rooms across 4 floors, each floor themed around a different story concept. Among hotels near Tokyo Disney Resort, it offers one of the most affordable price points. The 23:00 last bus back to the hotel is the most important detail to know before booking — if you miss it, you'll need a taxi. With only 8 parking spaces at ¥1,500/night (first-come), and the nearest convenience store 19 minutes on foot, guests who book here should do so with eyes open. This review covers strengths, weaknesses, and access from a local perspective. Prices and times are estimates based on 2025–2026 research.

## Best For

- **Guests who want the lowest possible price near TDR** — Among the most affordable hotels in the Maihama/Shin-Urayasu area; ideal for "Disney on a tight budget"
- **Guests who appreciate a unique story-themed small hotel** — Each of the 4 floors has a completely different story/world theme, creating an intimate boutique experience
- **Guests who don't need a late shuttle** — Can return to the hotel by the last bus at 23:00 from the parks

## May Not Suit

- **Guests who plan to stay in the park until late closing** — The last shuttle back to the hotel is 23:00; if the park closes at or after 22:00 and you stay until the end, a taxi may be needed
- **Guests who rely on in-hotel convenience stores** — Nearest convenience store is 19 min on foot; stock up before returning to the hotel or use food delivery
- **Guests arriving by car with more than 1 vehicle** — Only 8 parking spaces (¥1,500/night, first-come); contact the hotel in advance

## Access

### To Tokyo Disneyland (TDL)
Local bus from 'Horie-Chugakko-mae' to Maihama Station ~7 min (Y170), then Disney Resort Line to Tokyo Disneyland Station (no direct shuttle; last bus 23:00).

### To Tokyo DisneySea (TDS)
Local bus from 'Horie-Chugakko-mae' to Maihama Station ~7 min (Y170), then Disney Resort Line to Tokyo DisneySea Station (no direct shuttle; last bus 23:00).

### To JR Station
Access from Maihama Station or Shin-Urayasu Station (confirm route on official website or at check-in).

### To the Airport
From the nearest major bus stop or station, airport limousine service to Haneda and Narita (confirm details at the hotel).

### To Central Tokyo
Via Maihama Station (JR Keiyo Line) or Shin-Urayasu Station (JR Keiyo Line), approx. 15–20 min to Tokyo Station.

## Local Notes

The two things that define a stay at Four Stories Hotel — for better or worse — are **the affordable price** and **the 23:00 last shuttle.** These aren't independent: the reason the price is as low as it is partly reflects the location and shuttle constraints. For guests who plan their park day around being back at the hotel by 23:00, this is genuinely a good value option with a distinctive small-hotel atmosphere.

For guests who want to stay until park fireworks or late events, or need a late-night convenience run, the trade-offs become tangible. Know these ahead of time and this hotel can be a great find.
