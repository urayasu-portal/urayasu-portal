LAGENT HOTEL Tokyo Bay won the Rakuten Travel Silver Award in 2024 with consistently high guest ratings. An adjacent 24-hour convenience store, 190 parking spaces (first-come), rooms for up to 6 guests, and 4 coin laundry machines cover a wide range of practical needs. The return shuttle from TDR runs until approximately 21:00 — suitable for guests who don't need a very late shuttle back. No large communal bath. This review covers the hotel's strengths, weaknesses, and access. Prices and times are estimates based on 2025–2026 research.

## Best For

- **Guests arriving by car** — 190 first-come parking spaces (the most among Shin-Urayasu business hotels); a major advantage for driving families
- **Guests who want a 24-hour convenience store adjacent to the hotel** — Directly adjacent; accessible without going outside in most layouts
- **Large groups and families of up to 6** — Rooms for up to 6 guests available; practical for extended family trips
- **Guests who need coin laundry** — 4 machines in-house; less waiting than hotels with only 2

## May Not Suit

- **Guests who want to stay at the parks until late closing** — Return shuttle from TDR runs until approx. 21:00; for late park closings (22:00+), taxi or other alternatives needed
- **Guests wanting a large communal bath or natural hot spring** — No large communal bath
- **Guests who want the absolute latest shuttle service** — For later shuttles, consider Oriental Tokyo Bay or Brighton

## Access

### To Tokyo Disneyland (TDL)
Free hotel shuttle, approx. 25 min (return service until ~21:00).

### To Tokyo DisneySea (TDS)
Free hotel shuttle, approx. 25 min (return service until ~21:00).

### To JR Station
Confirm walking distance and bus options from Shin-Urayasu Station on the official website.

### To the Airport
From "Shin-Urayasu Station" bus stop (South Exit, stop H): Haneda approx. 40–45 min, ¥1,300 (Tokyo Airport Transport / Keiseibus Chiba West; advance reservation recommended during peak seasons). Narita also available.

### To Central Tokyo
From Shin-Urayasu Station, about 20 minutes to Tokyo Station on the JR Keiyo Line.

## Local Notes

LAGENT HOTEL's 190-space parking lot is locally notable — it's one of the largest at any business hotel in the Shin-Urayasu area. For families arriving by car (especially during peak periods when other hotels' lots fill quickly), this alone can be the deciding factor.

The 2024 Rakuten Silver Award rating reflects consistently high reviews on value, cleanliness, and service — worth noting for a hotel in this price range. The shuttle cut-off at approximately 21:00 is the main practical limitation; plan your park departure time accordingly.
