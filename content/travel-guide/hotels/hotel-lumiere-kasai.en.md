Hotel Lumiere Kasai is about a 2-minute walk from Tokyo Metro Tozai Line Kasai Station. It includes a free breakfast in the 1F lounge (6:30–10:00) and offers twin rooms with a microwave and mini kitchen, along with coin laundry on multiple floors — convenient for longer stays. The Tozai Line puts Urayasu one stop away and Otemachi about 20 minutes away. Prices and times are estimates based on 2025–2026 research.

## Best For

- **Guests who want breakfast before heading out** — Free breakfast in the 1F lounge (6:30–10:00)
- **Guests who want to be near the station** — 2 min from Tozai Line Kasai Station
- **Longer stays / light self-catering** — Twin rooms with mini kitchen; coin laundry on multiple floors

## May Not Suit

- **Guests who want a large bath** — No large bath (in-room bath only)
- **Guests with tall vehicles** — Mechanical tower parking with size limits (e.g., height up to 2.05 m)

## Access

### To Tokyo Disneyland (TDL)
Keisei bus from Kasai Station, approx. 20 min (15 min by car).

### To Tokyo DisneySea (TDS)
Keisei bus from Kasai Station, approx. 20 min (15 min by car).

### To the Nearest Station
About 2 min on foot from Tokyo Metro Tozai Line Kasai Station.

### To the Airport
About 50–60 min to Haneda Airport by train/bus connections.

### To Central Tokyo
About 20 min to Otemachi / 18 min to Nihonbashi on the Tozai Line (one stop to Urayasu).

## Facilities & Services
{{< hotel-facilities >}}

**Guest Rooms**
Single / double / twin (with microwave and mini kitchen).

**Breakfast**
Free breakfast in the 1F lounge (6:30–10:00): bread, egg dishes, salad, soup, etc.

**Coin Laundry**
On floors 5, 8 and 11 (washer ¥300 / dryer ¥200 per hour).

**Parking**
Mechanical tower parking, 50 spaces, ¥1,500/night (reservation; vehicle size limits).

**Luggage Storage**
Available before check-in and after check-out.

**Wi-Fi**
Free throughout.

**Large Bath**
None.
