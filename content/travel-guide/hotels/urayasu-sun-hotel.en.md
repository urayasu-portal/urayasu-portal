Urayasu Sun Hotel is a compact business hotel approximately 3 minutes on foot from Urayasu Station (Tokyo Metro Tozai Line). Free continental breakfast is served from 6:00am — the earliest breakfast option in the Urayasu Station area. A 24-hour front desk accepts arrivals until 0:30. Coin laundry on 2F (2 washers + 2 dryers), parking at ¥900/night (10 spaces, first-come), a day-use plan (¥8,800+), and weekly plans round out the practical offerings. This review covers strengths, weaknesses, and access. Prices and times are estimates based on 2025–2026 research.

## Best For

- **Guests who want the earliest breakfast in the Urayasu Station area** — Continental breakfast from 6:00am; head to the parks on a full stomach without waiting
- **Guests who need a late check-in** — 24-hour front desk accepts arrivals until 0:30; suitable for guests arriving on late-night trains or buses
- **Budget-conscious guests who want proximity to Urayasu Station** — Among the most affordable options within walking distance of Urayasu Station (3 min on foot)
- **Extended stay guests** — Weekly plans and day-use plans (¥8,800+) available for guests needing flexibility

## May Not Suit

- **Guests who want a quick shuttle to TDR** — No free shuttle; TDR access requires the local bus from Urayasu Station (approx. 25–30 min)
- **Guests wanting a large communal bath** — No large bath
- **Guests who need more than 10 parking spaces** — Only 10 first-come spaces at ¥900/night; contact in advance if parking is needed

## Access

### To Tokyo Disneyland
Keiseibus Chiba West from Urayasu Station, approx. 20 min (no dedicated shuttle).

### To Tokyo DisneySea
Keiseibus Chiba West from Urayasu Station, approx. 20 min (no dedicated shuttle).

### To JR Station
About 3 min on foot from Urayasu Station (Tokyo Metro Tozai Line). Transfer to JR lines via Nihonbashi, Otemachi, and connecting stations.

### To the Airport
No dedicated limousine stop. To Haneda: approx. 60 min via Urayasu → Nihonbashi → Toei Asakusa Line or Keikyu Line. To Narita: approx. 80–90 min via the same route.

### To Central Tokyo
Urayasu Station → Tokyo Metro Tozai Line: approx. 16–18 min to Nihonbashi/Otemachi.

## Local Notes

Urayasu Sun Hotel fits a specific profile: guests who want to use the Tozai Line rather than the Keiyo Line, need a 3-minute walk to the station, want free breakfast from 6:00am, and need late check-in capability. For that combination, it's one of the more practical options in the Urayasu Station area.

The parking rate of ¥900/night stands out — it's lower than most area hotels. The 10-space limit means advance contact is necessary; it's not a guarantee, but the rate itself is notable.
