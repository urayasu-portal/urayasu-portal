Mitsui Garden Hotel Prana Tokyo Bay is a TDR Partner Hotel offering a combination that's hard to find in this category: a **rooftop large bath with ocean views** (free for guests, 18:00–9:00), a **24-hour in-house convenience store**, and a **dedicated airport limousine stop on the premises**. Rooms accommodate up to 6 guests, and the shuttle from Shin-Urayasu Station runs at up to 10-minute intervals. This review covers the hotel's strengths, weaknesses, and access. Prices and times are estimates based on 2025–2026 research.

## Best For

- **Guests who want a rooftop ocean view bath after the parks — free of charge** — Rooftop large bath with sea views is included in the room rate (18:00–9:00); ideal for unwinding after a full park day
- **Guests who need a 24-hour in-house convenience store** — 24-hour convenience store inside the hotel; reliable late-night access for snacks and daily goods
- **Guests arriving directly from the airport with heavy luggage** — Dedicated limousine stop on the hotel premises; board and alight without navigating to a separate stop
- **Large groups of up to 6** — Room types for up to 6 guests available

## May Not Suit

- **Guests who want the quickest shuttle to TDR** — Shuttle to TDR from Shin-Urayasu takes approx. 20 min (same as other Shin-Urayasu Partner Hotels); not a walkable distance to parks
- **Guests who want a natural hot spring (not just a large bath)** — The rooftop bath is a large communal bath, not a natural hot spring; for natural hot spring, consider Emion

## Access

### To Tokyo Disneyland (TDL)
Partner Hotel free shuttle (no reservation), approx. 20 min (up to every 10 min).

### To Tokyo DisneySea (TDS)
Partner Hotel free shuttle (no reservation), approx. 20 min (up to every 10 min).

### To JR Station
Shuttle from Shin-Urayasu Station: approx. 20 min to the hotel (confirm schedule and stop location on official website). Or local bus from Shin-Urayasu Station South Exit.

### To the Airport
**Dedicated airport limousine stop on the hotel premises.** Haneda: approx. 40–45 min, ¥1,300. Narita: approx. 60 min, ¥2,900 (Tokyo Airport Transport / Keiseibus Chiba West; advance reservation recommended during peak seasons). No need to walk to Shin-Urayasu Station bus stop.

### To Central Tokyo
From Shin-Urayasu Station, about 20 minutes to Tokyo Station on the JR Keiyo Line.

## Local Notes

Mitsui Garden Hotel Prana's combination of **rooftop sea view bath + 24-hour in-house convenience store + on-premises limousine stop** is genuinely unusual among Partner Hotels. Each individual feature is available at some hotel somewhere, but having all three at one property in the Partner Hotel category is rare.

Within the four Shin-Urayasu Partner Hotels (Brighton, Oriental, Emion, Mitsui Garden): Brighton wins on station access, Oriental wins on shuttle frequency and Cinderella-themed rooms, Emion wins on natural hot spring, and Mitsui Garden wins on the rooftop bath + limousine access combination. The shuttle interval to Shin-Urayasu Station (~20 min, up to 10-min frequency) is the clearest access trade-off versus Brighton's 1-min deck connection.
