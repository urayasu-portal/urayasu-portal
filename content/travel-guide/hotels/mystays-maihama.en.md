MyStays Hotel Maihama is a compact 90-room hotel with a distinctive advantage: at approximately 6 minutes on foot to Tokyo DisneySea, it is the **closest non-Disney-Hotel property in Area B** to the park entrance. In-room foot massagers make it particularly comfortable after a full day of walking, and fresh-baked bread at breakfast is a draw in its own right. Check-out is 10:00. This review covers the hotel's strengths, weaknesses, and access. Prices and times are estimates based on 2025–2026 research.

## Best For

- **Guests who want to walk to TDS in the shortest time outside the Disney Hotel lineup** — Approx. 6-minute walk to TDS entrance; the shortest non-Disney-Hotel walking distance in Area B
- **Guests whose feet hurt after a full park day** — In-room foot massager in all 90 rooms; a small detail that makes a real difference
- **Budget-conscious guests who still want convenience** — Starting at approx. ¥5,000, with unusually close TDS proximity for the price range

## May Not Suit

- **Guests who need a late check-out** — 10:00 check-out is among the earliest in the area
- **Guests who need a 24-hour in-hotel store** — No in-house store; nearest convenience store is approx. 5 min on foot
- **Guests who want the Disney Hotel benefits (Happy Entry, character rooms)** — No Disney Hotel benefits; non-Disney brand

## Access

### To Tokyo Disneyland (TDL)
Free shuttle bus, or via Maihama Station (schedule on the official PC site).

### To Tokyo DisneySea (TDS)
~6-min walk to the TDS main gate; a free shuttle bus also runs daily.

### To JR Station
Maihama Station: approx. 10–15 min on foot or by local transport.

### To the Airport
From Maihama Station bus stop, airport limousine to Haneda: approx. 40 min, ¥1,300. Narita: approx. 75 min, ¥2,900.

### To Central Tokyo
From Maihama Station, about 15 minutes to Tokyo Station on the JR Keiyo Line.

## Local Notes

For guests who want to walk to TDS without paying Disney Hotel rates, MyStays Maihama has a practical claim: it's the closest hotel by walking distance to TDS outside the Disney-operated properties in the Area B zone. At ¥5,000+ starting, that proximity-per-yen ratio is hard to beat.

The 10:00 check-out and compact 90-room scale are trade-offs that come with the low price. For a two-night TDS-heavy trip where you want to minimize transit time and control costs, this hotel is often the answer that experienced budget TDS visitors arrive at.
