Royal Park Hotel The Maihama opened on February 13, 2026 — the largest new hotel near Tokyo Disney Resort in years, with 750 rooms. The rooms are themed around four oceans (Pacific, Atlantic, Indian, Arctic), an in-house 24-hour Lawson is on the ground floor, 10 coin laundry machines (with room availability viewable on the in-room TV) are in the building, and dedicated shuttle buses to TDL and TDS are provided. **However, this is not a TDR Official Hotel or Good Neighbor Hotel — Happy Entry early admission does not apply.** This review covers strengths, weaknesses, and access. Prices and times are estimates based on 2025–2026 research.

## Access

### To Tokyo Disneyland
Dedicated free shuttle looping Hotel-TDL-TDS, daily (schedule on the official site).

### To Tokyo DisneySea
Dedicated free shuttle, or ~15-min walk from the Disney Resort Line Tokyo DisneySea Station.

### To JR Station
Access to Maihama Station and the Keiyo Line (confirm route on official website).

### To the Airport
Access from Maihama Station bus stop or other stops in the area. Haneda and Narita airport limousine available (confirm stops and schedule before visiting).

### To Central Tokyo
From Maihama Station, about 15 minutes to Tokyo Station on the JR Keiyo Line.

**Guest Rooms (750 rooms, 4-seas theme)**
Four ocean themes across the rooms — Pacific, Atlantic, Indian, and Arctic — each with distinct design language. 750 rooms is the largest count among hotels near TDR (surpassing Hilton Tokyo Bay at 828 on a "newly built" comparison), though the full room range needs confirmation on the official website.

Lawson on 1F open 24 hours. A practical necessity for a hotel of this size — late-night shopping, breakfast pickup, and daily goods all available without leaving the building.

**Coin Laundry (10 machines)**
10 coin laundry machines — the most in the area. Availability viewable on the in-room TV, so no need to walk to the laundry room just to check.

**Dedicated Shuttle to TDL/TDS**
Dedicated shuttle to both parks (independent from TDR's official shuttle network). Not Happy Entry eligible, but shuttles run regularly.

## Local Notes

Royal Park Hotel The Maihama's opening in February 2026 is the most significant hotel addition near TDR in recent years. 750 rooms, 10 coin laundry machines, a 24-hour Lawson, and 394 parking spaces represent a combination of scale and amenities that simply wasn't available before February 2026.

The critical caveat is the **Happy Entry situation**: this hotel is not affiliated with TDR's Official Hotel or Good Neighbor Hotel network. Guests who plan their itinerary around 15-minute early park access should choose a Disney Hotel, Official Hotel, or Good Neighbor Hotel instead. For guests who don't care about Happy Entry and want a brand-new, well-equipped large hotel near TDR at the ¥25,000–¥60,000 range, this becomes a very strong contender — especially for driving families who need parking.
