This guide covers taxi stands and dispatch companies by station, flat-rate airport taxi fares, and tips for wheelchair-accessible and welfare taxis in the Urayasu, Shin-Urayasu, and Maihama areas.

Taxis are useful in many situations in Urayasu — after Tokyo Disney Resort closes, for hotel transfers in Shin-Urayasu, or for early-morning airport runs. The key is knowing the difference between **walking up to a taxi stand** and **calling or app-dispatching a taxi**.

> **Please note**
> Taxi fares, flat-rate airport fares, booking fees, pick-up fees, coverage areas, and dispatch conditions are subject to change. Before use, check the latest information on individual taxi company websites, the Chiba Taxi Association website, and official airport websites.
>
> Last verified: June 19, 2026

---

## Quick Summary: Which Method for Which Situation

| Situation | Check first | Recommended approach |
|---|---|---|
| Going from station to hotel | Location of taxi stand in front of station | Queue at the taxi stand |
| Going from hotel / accommodation to airport | Flat-rate fare, booking availability | Reserve in advance |
| Moving after TDR park closing | Stand congestion, alternative options | Allow extra time |
| Wheelchair / elderly / heavy luggage | UD taxi / welfare taxi availability | Enquire and book in advance |

---

## When Is a Taxi Useful?

While many destinations in Urayasu are accessible by train or bus, taxis are especially handy in these situations:

| Situation | Why a taxi helps |
|---|---|
| Small children | Avoids transfers and walking |
| Large suitcases | Direct from station to hotel |
| Rainy weather | Reduces outdoor exposure |
| After TDR park closing | Group transfer to hotel or station |
| Early / late flights | When trains and buses are infrequent |
| Bay-side hotel in Shin-Urayasu | Some hotels are far from the station |
| Wheelchair users / mobility concerns | Can reduce physical strain with advance booking |

Park closing time, rain, and early morning airport runs are particularly high-demand periods. For guaranteed service, book in advance or have backup options ready.

## Station Stand vs. Dispatched Taxi

| Location | How to use | Key point |
|---|---|---|
| Station taxi stand | Queue at the stand | May be separate from reservation system |
| Hotel / accommodation / facility | Phone or app dispatch | Pick-up and booking fees may apply |
| Airport-bound | Pre-booking recommended | Confirm flat-rate eligibility |
| Wheelchair / heavy luggage | Enquire in advance | Vehicle type or wagon may need to be specified |

Maihama Resort Cab's official information states that reservations are not accepted at taxi stands including Urayasu, Shin-Urayasu, and Maihama stations. For early morning flights or large luggage, booking in advance from your hotel is more reliable than queuing at the station.

## Flat-Rate Airport Taxis

Some taxi companies offer flat-rate fares from Urayasu to Haneda and Narita airports.

> **Please note**
> Flat-rate fares, booking conditions, expressway tolls, booking fees, and vehicle upgrade fees vary by company. Always check the official website before booking.

### Maihama Resort Cab Flat-Rate Fares

| Destination | Standard | Late Night / Early Morning | Disability Discount | Late Night + Disability |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| Haneda Airport | ¥9,500 | ¥11,500 | ¥8,500 | ¥10,000 |
| Narita Airport | ¥24,500 | ¥29,000 | ¥22,000 | ¥26,000 |

Additional charges may apply:

- **Expressway toll (approximate, off-peak):** Haneda direction ¥810, Narita direction ¥1,750
- **Booking fee:** ¥500 per booking
- **Wagon vehicle surcharge:** ¥1,300 per booking

### Airport-to-Urayasu Flat-Rate Availability

Flat-rate taxi fares from Urayasu to airports have been confirmed with multiple companies, but **flat-rate availability and boarding procedures for trips from the airport back to Urayasu vary by company and booking method**. Flat-rate taxi services at Haneda and Narita airports are primarily directed toward central Tokyo and Kanagawa destinations. If returning from the airport to Urayasu, confirm directly with your chosen company.

| Item | Why it matters |
|---|---|

| Stroller folding requirement | Depends on vehicle size |
| Number of suitcases | Trunk space may be limited |
| Wagon vehicle availability | May incur additional fee |
| Early / late booking availability | Slots may be limited |

### UD Taxis

UD (Universal Design) taxis are designed to be accessible for wheelchair users, elderly passengers, and stroller users. However, immediate availability of UD taxis in the Urayasu area cannot be guaranteed. If you need a wheelchair-accessible vehicle or have large luggage, contact taxi companies in advance rather than looking for one on the spot.

## FAQ

### Are there taxi stands at Urayasu, Shin-Urayasu, and Maihama stations?

Yes, there are stands in front of each station. Check the signs at the station for exact locations.

### Can I pick up a pre-booked taxi at the station taxi stand?

Policies differ by company. Maihama Resort Cab's official information states that reservations cannot be accepted at station taxi stands. For station stands, queue up. For dispatched service, book from your hotel or accommodation.

### Can I take a flat-rate taxi from Urayasu to Haneda Airport?

Yes, some companies offer flat-rate fares from Urayasu to Haneda. Fares and booking conditions vary — check the official website before use.

### Can I take a flat-rate taxi from Urayasu to Narita Airport?

Some companies offer flat-rate fares. Narita is farther away so the fare is higher, but it can be a convenient choice when you have heavy luggage or an early morning departure.

### Are flat-rate fares available for the return trip from the airport?

Flat-rate availability for airport-to-Urayasu trips varies by company and booking method. Confirm with your chosen company before travel.

### Are there wheelchair-accessible taxis?

Urayasu City's official website lists welfare taxi operators (see table above). Conditions and booking policies vary by operator — always confirm in advance.

### What if I have a stroller or heavy luggage?

Standard taxis can usually handle strollers and luggage, but you may need to specify a wagon-type vehicle for larger loads. Specify luggage details when booking.

### Can I get a taxi immediately after Disney park closing?

Demand spikes after closing and in rainy weather, so wait times may occur. For reliable service, allow extra time or check hotel shuttles, the Disney Resort Line, and local buses.

## Summary

In Urayasu, think of taxis in two ways: **walking up to a stand at the station** and **calling or using an app from your hotel**.

- **Maihama Station** — Convenient after TDR closing or for direct hotel transfers
- **Shin-Urayasu Station** — Handy for bay-area hotel transfers; some hotels are far from the station
- **Urayasu Station** — For city-center hotels and Tozai Line area

For Haneda and Narita airports, some companies offer flat-rate fares. Fares and booking conditions vary — always check the official website. If you need a wheelchair or welfare taxi, or have heavy luggage, contact operators in advance rather than looking on the day.
